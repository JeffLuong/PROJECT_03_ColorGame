Move your tile to the diamond. However, you must be the same color as the diamond when you get there!

Use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate through the board. R restarts the game, U undos your move, I redos your move!

Use the circles to help you decide which direction to go! The circles are "previews" of the new color you will make if you go to that direction! Good luck!
